\subsection{Algorithms}

The algorithms were chosen to span a range of time complexities and computational patterns, including CPU-bound, memory-bound, and branching-heavy workloads. The six algorithms used in this study are summarized in Table~\ref{tab:algorithms}.


\begin{table}[h]

    \centering
    \caption{Algorithms, their time complexities and computational patterns.}
    \label{tab:algorithms}
    \scriptsize
    \begin{tabular}{lll}
        \hline
        \textbf{Algorithm} & \textbf{Time Complexity} & \textbf{Pattern} \\
        \hline
        Summation                      & $O(n)$            & Linear \\
        Binary Search                  & $O(\log n)$       & Logarithmic \\
        Merge Sort                     & $O(n \log n)$     & Divide \& Conquer \\
        Breadth-First Search (BFS)     & $O(V + E)$        & Graph Traversal \\
        Hash Table                     & $O(1)$ average    & Constant-time lookup \\
        Matrix Multiplication          & $O(n^3)$          & Dense arithmetic \\
        \hline
    \end{tabular}
    
    
\end{table}
