Before presenting results, we report the integrity of the collected data. The benchmark runner produced a CSV file containing 12,420 rows, exactly 115 runs for each of the 108 cells. All 108 cells returned a consistent checksum across all 115 runs, confirming that ever implementation produced identical output on every invocation. The carbon intensity column contained exactly two distinct values (300 and 306 gCO$_2$/kWh), consistent with the planned 30 minute refresh cadence and the expeceted stability of the overnight New York grid. The total energy recorded across all 12,420 runs was \textbf{86,009.7~J}, producing a tatal estimated carbon footprint of \textbf{7.28~gCO$_2$e} for the entire experiment.
