As technology continues to evolve, software becomes an increasingly central part of our lives. From the apps on our phones to the software running our cars, it is everywhere. But have you ever stopped to think about the environmental impact of all this software? Every time a piece of code runs, it triggers energy usage inside the CPU, GPU, and other hardware components. This subsequent use of software adds up to the carbon emissions a device generates (Thoughtworks Carbon Intensity report). The carbon footprint of software is a growing concern, as it contributes to climate change and has a significant impact on our planet.
