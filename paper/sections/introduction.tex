As the tech continues to evolve, software becomes an increaingly central part of our lives. From the apps on our phones to the software running our cars, it's everywhere. But have you ever stopped to think about the environmental impact of all this software? Everytime a  piece of code runs, it triggers energy usage inside the CPU, GPU, and other hardware components. This subsequent usage of software adds up to the carbon emission a device generates. The carbon footprint of software is a growing concern, as it contributes to climate change and has a significant impact on our planet.
